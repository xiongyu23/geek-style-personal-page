Careers

Join our team of quantitative researchers and engineers building next-generation trading systems based on advanced mathematical models from dynamical systems, symbolic dynamics, point process theory, stochastic optimal control, and computational methods. We value intellectual curiosity, technical excellence, and collaborative problem-solving.

Available Positions

Senior Quantitative Researcher

We're seeking a senior quantitative researcher with strong mathematical foundations in stochastic processes, point process theory, or related fields, and practical experience in algorithmic trading strategy development. The ideal candidate will bridge pure mathematical research with practical financial applications.

Requirements:
  • PhD in Mathematics, Physics, Statistics, or related quantitative field
  • 3+ years experience in quantitative finance or algorithmic trading
  • Proficiency in Python, C++, or Rust for numerical computing and backtesting
  • Deep understanding of statistical modeling, time series analysis, and point process theory
  • Experience with order book dynamics modeling or high-frequency trading research
  • Familiarity with Hawkes processes or other point process models is a strong plus
  • Knowledge of machine learning techniques applied to financial markets is desirable
Responsibilities:
  • Develop and validate quantitative trading strategies based on mathematical models of market dynamics
  • Apply point process theory and stochastic optimal control to model order book behavior and market microstructure
  • Analyze high-frequency market data to identify alpha opportunities using advanced statistical methods
  • Collaborate with engineering team on strategy implementation and optimization
  • Conduct rigorous backtesting and performance analysis of quantitative models
  • Contribute to the development of our API platform for signal delivery
  • Explore and integrate machine learning techniques into existing modeling frameworks

Rust Software Engineer (Low-Latency Systems)

We need a skilled Rust engineer to build and maintain our high-performance, low-latency trading infrastructure. The ideal candidate has experience with ultra-low latency systems and understands the mathematical requirements of quantitative trading algorithms.

Requirements:
  • 3+ years professional Rust development experience
  • Experience with high-performance computing and low-latency systems (nanosecond/microsecond scale)
  • Understanding of financial markets, order book mechanics, and trading systems
  • Knowledge of network programming, concurrent systems, and cache-efficient algorithm design
  • Familiarity with quantitative finance concepts and numerical computing
  • Experience with C++ and performance optimization is a plus
Responsibilities:
  • Develop low-latency trading execution systems in Rust
  • Optimize data processing pipelines for high-frequency market data
  • Implement risk management and monitoring systems with minimal latency overhead
  • Collaborate with researchers to implement mathematical trading models efficiently
  • Design and implement the API infrastructure for our quantitative signal platform
  • Ensure system reliability and performance under extreme market conditions

Quantitative Trading Intern

We offer a highly technical internship program for students interested in quantitative finance and algorithmic trading. Interns will work closely with our research team on mathematical modeling of financial markets and strategy development.

Requirements:
  • Currently pursuing a degree in Mathematics, Computer Science, Physics, or related quantitative field
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with a foundation in calculus, linear algebra, and probability
  • Programming experience in Python (NumPy, Pandas, SciPy) or similar language
  • Basic understanding of financial markets and trading concepts
  • Interest in dynamical systems, stochastic processes, or applied mathematics
  • Ability to work independently and in small teams
Internship Details:
  • 3-6 month paid internship (remote or New York-based)
  • Hands-on experience with real market data and quantitative research methodologies
  • Mentorship from senior researchers and engineers with PhD-level mathematical backgrounds
  • Exposure to Hawkes process modeling, order book dynamics, and high-frequency trading systems
  • Opportunity to contribute to our API platform development
  • Opportunity for full-time position for top performers

How to Apply

To apply for any position, please submit your resume, cover letter, and any relevant work samples or research papers. For quantitative roles, please include a brief description of your mathematical background and any relevant research or projects.

Email Application

Send your application materials to: me@xiongyu.org

Please include the position title in the subject line (e.g., "Application: Senior Quantitative Researcher").

Required Materials

1. Updated resume/CV

2. Cover letter explaining your interest and qualifications

3. Academic transcripts (for internships or junior positions)

4. Research papers, code samples, or portfolio (if applicable)

Application Process

1. Initial screening of application materials

2. Technical assessment (mathematical/coding problems relevant to the role)

3. Interviews with team members (research/technical focus)

4. On-site or remote final interview with founder (for full-time positions)

We aim to respond to all applications within 1-2 weeks of submission.
